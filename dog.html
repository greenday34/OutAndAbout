Oceans Shores Off-Leash Dog Area

Click on the start button to view the video.

Almost every city or town has a dog park. Some of the more memorable ones are at Silverdale, Sequim and Port Angeles. The off-leash beach at Ocean Shores is a must visit for you and your pooches.

Trails

There are many dog friendly trails in the National Forests, State, City and County Parks.

Just out and about

A great place to walk the puppies is along the waterfront in Poulsbo. You can make it a long or short walk.
